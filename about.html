I am a visual artist working with figurative painting and drawing.

My practice explores images through states of suspension, ambiguity, and incomplete action. Figures, gestures, and spaces remain slightly misaligned, forming perceptual situations that stay open and unresolved.

Before developing my artistic practice, I studied visual culture and image theory, focusing on contemporary theories of perception. This background continues to shape my interest in attention, spatial tension, and relations within the image.
